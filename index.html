CI/CD integration

Gate your pipeline.
On your terms.

Scans exit 0 by default — never breaks your build until you’re ready. Opt into enforcement incrementally.

FlagBehaviourExit
(none)Report only0
--fail-on-confidence HIGHFail on HIGH2
--fail-on-confidence MEDIUMFail on MEDIUM+2
--fail-on-cost 50Fail if waste ≥ $50/mo2
--fail-on-findingsFail on any finding2

Use cases

One tool, three workflows.

01One-time audit
Run in CloudShell against any account. See all findings with cost estimates in under 60 seconds — no local setup, no agents.
$ cleancloud scan --provider aws --all-regions
02Scheduled hygiene
Weekly cron or CI run. Catch drift between deployments before idle resources compound into hundreds per month.
$ cleancloud scan --provider gcp --project my-project
03CI enforcement gate
Block PRs when waste crosses your threshold. Engineers fix it at review time, not production. FinOps meets DevOps at the pull request.
$ cleancloud scan --fail-on-cost 100
